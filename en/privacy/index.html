Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 6, 2025

At Iron Riders ("we", "our", or "app"), we value your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you use our mobile application, how we use it, and how we protect it.

1.2 Location Data

Since our app's core function is group ride tracking, we collect your location data:

About Location Permission

  • Foreground Location: We access your location when you're actively using the app.
  • Background Location: During live rides, we track your location even when the app is in the background. This is necessary for other participants to see you on the map.
  • Location Precision: GPS coordinates (latitude, longitude), speed, and heading.
  • Update Frequency: Location updates every 5 seconds during live rides.

1.3 Usage Data

  • Ride statistics (distance, duration, average speed)
  • Earned badges and points
  • App usage logs

1.4 Device Information

  • Device type and operating system
  • Push notification token
  • App version

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information for:

  • Service Delivery: Group ride tracking, club management, and social features
  • Safety: SOS emergency system, stop requests, and location sharing
  • Communication: Ride notifications, club announcements, and important updates
  • Improvement: Enhancing the app and user experience
  • Statistics: Analyzing ride data and providing personal statistics

4. Data Security

We take the following measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption: All data transfers are encrypted via HTTPS
  • Authentication: Secure JWT token-based authentication
  • Secure Storage: Sensitive information (tokens) is stored securely on your device
  • Access Control: Only authorized systems can access your data

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for the following periods:

  • Account Info: As long as your account is active
  • Ride Data: Indefinitely for statistics (anonymized)
  • Location Data: Real-time data deleted at end of live ride, ride summary retained

6. Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • Access: Learn what data we store about you
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Portability: Download your data
  • Objection: Object to data processing

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from users under 18. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

8. Location Permission Details

Why Background Location Permission is Required

Iron Riders is a group ride application. During live rides:

  • Other participants need to see your location
  • The leader needs to track the entire group on the map
  • Your location must be shared instantly in SOS situations

Therefore, location tracking is required even when the app is in the background during rides. Background location is not used outside of rides.
